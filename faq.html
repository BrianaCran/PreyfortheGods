F.A.Q

1. What platforms is Prey for the Gods being released on? Will you release this game on PS4? Xbox One? Wii U?

At this time Prey for the Gods is targeted for Steam, PS4 & Xbox One release. If a $400k stretch goal is met on Kickstarter we will also release on Mac.
Update: the stretch goal was met and it will be released for Mac!

2. When are you intending to release?

In Kickstarter backers hands by Dec 2017 on Steam. Consoles and Mac will come post Steam launch.

3. How big is No Matter Studios?

No Matter Studios is 3 developers. Brian Parnell, Hung-Chien Liao, and Tim Wiese. If you like to know more about us individually please visit our studio site.

4. Do you have a need for sound/music?

Not at this time. Thank you.

5. I'm a YouTube streamer can I have a free copy to review?

At this time, we are not giving out free keys or access to the game. When/If we do we'll let you know. Please, sign up for our newsletter where we'll make a later announcement.

6. What engine are you using?

We are using Unity 5.

7. Is the trailer CGI? or Ingame?

The trailer is 100% ingame, and shows real gameplay. Yes, the climbing is real.

8. How many bosses do you plan to have?

Prey for the Gods may contain 5 - 8 Bosses depending on the level of funding reached on Kickstarter.

9. Will this game have multiplayer?

That is something we are actively discussing and reviewing. When we've made our decision we'll announce in our newsletter.

9. How long have you worked on this?

Approx. since Sept 2014.
